FITProject is a a website containing a handful of tools to help user get and stay fit. Here you can keep track of your running and cycling records, body measurements over time, manage training plans and diet. All of these are available under the calendars section from the menu above. Additionally, you can also find here an interval timer that you can configure for your training and use directly while performing popular high intensity interval training (HIIT). Body mass index (BMI) calculator is also available. Please register or log in to gain access to all features.

This website was created as a final project for CS50 Web Programming with Python and Javascript course.
